001 — Origin
BORN FROM
FRUSTRATION.

Drift started in 2019 when two friends got tired of the same thing: coffee shops that were more interested in looking good on Instagram than actually making good coffee.

We wanted something different. A place with exposed concrete and mismatched chairs. A place where your barista knows the farm your coffee came from. A place where you could sit for 6 hours with a laptop and not feel judged.

That place is Drift.

002 — Manifesto
WE
BELIEVE
IN
COFFEE.
Coffee is a Craft

Not a commodity. Every step — from the farmer who grew the beans, to the roaster who profiled the batch, to the barista who dialed in the shot — matters. We respect the entire chain.

Atmosphere Is Not Decoration

We didn't hire an interior designer. We built this place by hand over three weeks. Every scratch on the counter is a decision we made. The raw concrete isn't aesthetic — it's honest.

Community Over Content

We don't care about your follower count. We care about whether you remember the barista's name. We've hosted local art shows, underground record swaps, and coding workshops in this space. That's what we're about.

Transparency is Non-Negotiable

We tell you exactly where every bean comes from, who grew it, and when it was roasted. If we ever can't answer those questions, we won't serve the coffee.
